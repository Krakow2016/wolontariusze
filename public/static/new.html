Why?

The volunteer job is often considered as a cheap labor and we want to change it. We are building the Mountain of Good for a strong and independent community of volunteers. Thanks to our portal, we want to inspire the other people to help each other. ohn Paul II during the pilgrimage in Lyon (France) said: Do not concentrate on your weaknesses and doubts .... Get up, straighten up and go

What we do?

We meet, we plan and develop an improved version of the portal, all with one purpose: to support and build community, which is a bridge that connects the active environment of the Church in Poland and in the world.

As the volunteers of the World Youth Day Krakow 2016 we have gained experience in the design of Volunteering Plus, coordination of WYD events and a huge desire to share and multiply the good. That’s why we want still create the portal “Mountain of Good” what was for the WYD Krakow 2016 and continue to grow and serve all people with passion, sensitive to others, building their lives on Christian values.

For Whom?

  • Do you have an idea, but you don't know how to make it happen?
  • Do you want to work actively in the community, the parish?
  • Do you want to look on your group of friends again, to discover their potentials and the needs of others?
  • Are you a leader?
  • Or maybe you would like to be, but you lack faith that everything you need is already in you?
  • Do you want to build the good around yourself, gain new inspirations and share them?

The Mountain of Good is the answer to your needs!

What is Mountain of Good?

  • It's an backgroung in which needs meet opportunities.
  • Is the answer to how smoothly from college go in the world of work.
  • Is a portal that helps coordinate the work on the projects arising from the real needs of fulfilment of deeds of mercy to others.
  • It's a place that teaches project management – it takes possibility to build and carry out a new project step by step, and to take part in an already existing one.
  • This is a practical school for building teams and teamwork, one of the skills the most currently desired by employers.
  • The community of volunteers (WYD and not only), who profess to similar values: respect,responsibility and integrity.
  • An opportunity for spiritual formation, participate in retreats and days of recollection.

How does it work?
